Privacy Policy

Qaniteen does not collect, store, or share any personal information or user data directly. However, Qaniteen uses the Facebook SDK to collect anonymous installation and basic usage analytics, solely to help us improve user experience and app performance. No personal information (such as name, email, or contact details) is accessed or stored by Qaniteen. The Facebook SDK may collect device identifiers and general usage data to provide analytics services. This information is not linked to your personal identity and is not used for advertising or personalized marketing within Qaniteen. For more details, please see Facebook’s Data Policy. Qaniteen does not require sign-in or registration, and the core features work offline. Internet access is occasionally needed for version checking and analytics updates. If Qaniteen adds features in the future that require collecting personal information, this privacy policy will be updated accordingly, and users will be notified within the app and on our website.

Contact

If you have any questions about our privacy policy, please contact us at support@qaniteen.com.
